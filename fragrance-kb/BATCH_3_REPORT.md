  that it actually sits closer to **Lattafa Khamrah**. Both edges are stored (INSPIRED_BY Angels'
  Share + SIMILAR_DNA Khamrah) so the graph reflects reality rather than the marketing.

  close." It is stored as SIMILAR_DNA (not INSPIRED_BY/clone) with low confidence.
- **Mahd Al Dhahab**: the only cited comparison is in-house (Lattafa Teriaq Intense); no designer
  target is confirmed, so it is ORIGINAL_DNA with that note in the evidence field.
- Web-search quota was hit mid-batch; a few flanker pyramids (Aariz base, Aariz L'Absolu, Ash'aa
  Amethyst/Blanc, Hypnotic Vanilla) are recorded at accord/family level and flagged `needs_review`
  for a note-level top-up later. All 30 still carry accords, so none are "awaiting data" blanks.
- The 9 "awaiting scent data" placeholders from Batch 2 (7 unverified Rayhaan names + Hawas Rouge +
  Yanis) are unchanged and still excluded from user-facing use.
